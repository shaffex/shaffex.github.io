datepicker is a container that stacks its children vertically.



```xml
<body>
    <form>
    	<datepicker key="myDate" value="20240822T173000Z">Select date</datepicker>
    	<datepicker key="myDatemiw" value="19770822T102030Z">Select date2</datepicker>
    </form>
</body>
```
