# Color Invert

**Description**

Inverts the colors of a view.

**Example**

```xml
<body>
    <vstack>
        <zstack>
            <rectangle foregroundColor="red"/>
            <text>RED color</text>
        </zstack>
        <zstack>
            <rectangle foregroundColor="red" colorInvert=""/>
            <text>Inverted RED color</text>
        </zstack>
    </vstack>
</body>
```
